TUA Program Requirements

Apeldoorn

  • An M.Th. equivalent to the M.Th. in the Netherlands system of education.
  • Proof that the student can conduct self-driven research that will end in a dissertation.
  • be able to spend at least 10 hours per week on their PhD research;
  • apply before April 1st of the calendar year in which they wish to start the program (starting September 1)
  • schedule an interview with one of our professors.
  • Expected to participate in the Graduate Week from 20-26 June of every year
  • An annual fee of € 675 (fee 2023/2024, there may be annual corrections)
  • Additionally, the legal tuition fee in the year of promotion (€ 2,314 for 2023/2024)

Other Requirements

  • a Curriculum Vitae (CV) - both personal and academic
  • a motivational letter in which you describe shortly why you would like to pursue
    your PhD studies in Apeldoorn.
  • a copy or scan of your previous academic certificates
  • a copy or scan of your passport
  • a passport photo
  • in the event that you wish to live in the Netherlands: a financial plan (the Dutch immigration
    service requires you to prove that you have a source of livelihood/income during your stay in
    the Netherlands (e.g. stipend; church support; personal support)
  • a scan of English language test results, i.e. one of the following: [IELTS - Academic] 6,5;
    [TOEFL iBT] 79; TOEFL cBT 213 (minimal scores as indicated)
  • your thesis (highest degree) and/or two recent (not older than 5 years) representative
    theological papers written in (or translated into) English
  • two letters of recommendation from professors of the institution you graduated from, or
    from earlier academic supervisors.
