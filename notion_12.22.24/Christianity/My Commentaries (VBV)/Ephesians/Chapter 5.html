Chapter 5

1: how are we ought to follow God’s example if we have never seen Him? How can we follow his steps if He has never been a human and He is way above human beings fro them to imitate Him? The only possible solution here is that He could set us an example by becoming like one of us. Walking like us, eating like us, suffering like us, living like us, breathing like us. The only explanation for this verse is the Lord Jesus! Jesus is God!

1: we ought to follow His example because we are loved children not because we are afraid of the punishment. You do good in response to God’s goodness nor in response to God’s judgement. That’s the real fear of God.

2: Why is it that immediately after telling us to walk in love he moves to the subject of pain? Because if you walk in the way of love with God it’ll cost you my friend! You will suffer. So count the cost. Before you say I do.

3: why do we so many time thing that we are only sinning immorally if we are indulging in sexual sins via physical contact? That’s not what we are told by Paul. He says not even a hint!! Or of any kind in many translations. This goes as far as improper flirting. I don’t care if she’s your girlfriend or he’s your boyfriend or if you guys are engaged and getting married the next day! You don’t flirt immorally!

9: We are called to produce justice, not to execute justice. #YLW #DR

10: Not which things are not bad, but which ones please the Lord. Yes many times you’ll want to get away with the fact that it is not a bad thing or that it is not a sin. But even if it is not a sin, as a matter of fact, even if is a commendable duty among Gods people, are you sure that’s His will for your life? Are you sure you’re not going left when He told you right? Are you sure that you’re but running a red light? Determine which things please Him! Because the things you’re doing might please Him in other individual’s life but not in yours. He might be pleased with someone simply serving the church by working as a greeter and never stand behind a microphone, but if He’s called you to preach you better be sure He won’t be pleased with you doing that. Determine what things please Him. Don’t just assume!

11: Oh I know this is not popular. I know Hollywood doesn’t give out Grammies for telling people what’s wrong. But this another verse where pail tells us “do” rather than “don’t do” the Christian life my friend is more about doing than not doing. Yes you think that simply not participating is cool with you. But not so with God, He tells you expose them!!!

15: He’s not saying be very careful how you behave outside of your house or in public platforms, but how you live. The Christian life is not a dot in a circle. Rather the dot is your life and Christ the circle. He wants to sorrowing your life. Listen, if you’re just going to carry Him to church, work, and school but don’t allow him into your house please don’t even carry Him at all. He wants you to be a Christian at work as much as He does at home. That’s why this book contains instructions on how to act from the moment you wake up and go to bed to even the time you sleep.

19: The greatest songs that I’ve heard from any musician have always been the ones they have written in the midst of hell. Is it not funny that we hear that David has been writing songs as a shepherd boy and at the palace of Saul but we don’t have any of those recorded? However, We do have one when a king’s army chased him out of pure envy. we do have the one his heart composed when he was caught in sin. We do have the one his heart sang when his baby passed. We do have one where his own son rebels against him and his back to persecution. We do have the one where that same son is murdered. In my opinion that’s the best genre God’s Record Production makes

21: When we offend or hurt a brother or sister, we are not only attacking their identity as human beings, we are directing our Lord, who lives in them. If we saw the face of our Great Lord in place of theirs, we would be less prompt to do anything that may cause harm to them. We cannot hide behind the excuse that they did an act that merited the attack. An attack is never excused for a child of God. We must not forget that no one of us is perfect. Attempting to correct their imperfection of wrongdoing, with our imperfection of retaliation, is like trying to put out a fire with gasoline. #YLW #DR

25: He’s not saying lead your wife. But love your wife. I know they both start with an L but don’t get them confused.

29: Unless we first learn how to forgive ourselves we will find it impossible to forgive our partners. Loving ourselves is not the vain and selfish love that the world teaches, typically pact in the phrase “do what makes you happy.” Loving ourselves is nurturing our spirit, soul, and body in a form that renders us closer to our Lord, even if this means looking passed our mistakes and allowing ourselves to be loved. However difficult this may be. Many of us do not know how to love not because we do not want to learn, but because we have refused this gift to ourselves. Any Christian who does not allow themselves to be loved by God, will do a very poor job at loving others and letting themselves be loved (1 Jn. 4:11). #RD #DR

32: If you don’t think that the relationship of Christ and the church is a mystery maybe you’re too holy and haven’t messed up much in life. But when you have messed up just as me and God is still chasing you. When you have turned your back on God and spit Him in the face. When you’ve kicked Him out of your heart and shut the door on Him. When you live a life as reckless and sinful as I have and He still pours His blessings on you, then my friend, you’ll know it’s a mystery. Perhaps you’re a good moral citizen and have always been a good Christian. Perhaps your cute, intelligent, talented, and successful and therefore it makes sense to you. But I, I cannot say the same. The love, mercy, and grace of God are definitely a mystery in my life.
