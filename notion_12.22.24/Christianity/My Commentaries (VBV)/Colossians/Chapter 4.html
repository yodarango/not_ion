2: Praying longer, more intentionally, and selecting the most uninterrupted time of our day to practice it are all part of persevering in prayer. I should strive to build my prayer on an environment characterized by uninterrupted attention and keen focus. He deserves the best and most productive time of my day. However, to all these characteristics there is a threshold. I can only pray so long and my mind can only focus so much. Therefore, the idea that Paul has in mind goes beyond the environment in which I exercise the privilege of prayer. Paul seems to be interested primarily with the content of prayer rather than with its environment. His second sentence “being alert in it with thanksgiving” alludes to the growth of character in prayer, rather than the enhancement of the technicalities. It is much easier to learn the discipline of early morning prayer of 3 hours than it is to be thankful when the world is crumbling down and God seems to ditch us in our prayer room. The Holy Spirit wants me to persevere in prayer by learning how to remain thankful even when my world is in pieces such that my humanity refuses to find anything to be thankful for. He wants to persevere in prayer by praying even when thing are so great in life that I cannot find a thing to pray for. #BL #DR
