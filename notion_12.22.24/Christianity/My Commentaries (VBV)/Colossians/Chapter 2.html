Chapter 2

1: My spiritual intersession should not be limited to those that surround me. I am obligated to pray even for those brethren that I do not they exist and who might never know that I prayed for them. #BL #DR

2: I can’t have a revelation of who God is while refusing to love His people. I will come to know that the more I love the more He will make Himself known to me. If I am ever wondering how wide my knowledge of God is, I must not look at how much Scripture I can recite of memory or how many hours I spend in spiritual disciplines and ministerial endeavors. I must only look inside my heart and find out how how much I love for my brethren. My knowledge of God will never be greater than my love for other believers. #RD#DR

4: I think what Paul is saying here is, do you like to learn? are you of a complex thinking and like to analyze the Important matters in life? Do you enjoy being knowledgeable and discovering more about life and its meaning? then follow Christ. In Him are all the answers. If you my friend, have lost your faith due to a good philosophical or scientific argument, in my own opinion you digged into extrange wells before drinking more from the fountain of the living water. It is a foolish thing to attempt to study the meaning of life if you haven’t found it. First make sure you have a solid relatioship with Christ! #LB #DR

6: Christianity does not end in the utterance of a mere statement, Christianity is a daily walk my friend. You must continue to live up to your confession [Rom 10:9] #GR #DR

7: Once again, Paul mentions his secret weapon to a strong relationship with God, thankfulness. Oh what a sad moment when a Christian becomes so accustomed to the goodness of God that they start living with, many a times, an unintentional mindset that is God’s responsibility to be good to us and thus, since we deem ourselves worthy of His goodness forget to thank Him and remember that every inhale we take in is an evidence of His Grace. Are you struggling to keep your faith and mantain a faithful walk with the Lord? well how about you trade your "why, God?" for a "thank you Lord?" #GR #DR

15: The devil thought He was humiliating Him, when in reality it was the other way around! He was humiliating him at the cross. God uses what the world and the devil consider disgracetul to humiliate them and at the same time glorify Him. Therefor if you currently find yourself in the midst of a struggle, hold on tight to Christ my beloved, God will use this trial for His Glory, your victory, and his Humiliation. #DB #DR

18: False visions huh? Sounds like all this TV “apostles” hearing God’s voice, Seeing Angels left and right, Predicting the future, and more #BR #DR

23: See, the problem of religion is that regardless it copious laws about staying away from fleshly desires it gives no power to control those same desires. That is something that only the Spirit of our Lord Jesus Christ can do. #DB #DR

21: Christianity is not a set of rules that you are given at the enrollment into God’s family, Christianity is a life You live guided by His Holy Spirit day by day. For when you come to God He gives you a role, not rules. The basis of obedience in Christianity is not Do’s and Dont’s , rather is want’s and wont’s. You do things because you want and you dont do things because you can but you just won’t. #GR #DR

23: Any pious act, regardless of how Godly it may appear, turns into legalism the moment I allow it to plant a seed of pride in my heart. By thinking that I am better than my brother or sister because I do something they are not willing to, I automatically become worse. Any principle or discipline that we resolve to adopt must be done from conviction, not competition. It is impossible to be Godlier than others when that becomes my goal. My goal should not be to become better than my brother or sister, my goal should be to become better than I was yesterday. I am my own measurement of growth and the standard is Christ (Gal. 6:4-5). #YWL #DR
