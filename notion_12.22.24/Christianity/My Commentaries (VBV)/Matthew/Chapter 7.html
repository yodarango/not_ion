11: The mind of a small child cannot understand the dangers of bouncing their ball on the side of the road. They can only understand their father is being unreasonable for not letting them have fun. They might kick and yell that they are unloved as they are denied this, in their eyes, thrilling diversion. But the dad knows. His mind can understand and his eyes can see the dangers hidden to them. A good father will do anything to protect his child, even if that might seem cruel to the tiny human. Similarly, good only knows how to give good things to His children, and He will answer our prayers only with that which is eternally beneficial for us. God knows the character of His children. He knows what to give to each one of them. To some, playing on the side of the road looks like a long life, therefore He will take them home early (Isa. 57:1-2), to some it may look like riches, so He will give them just enough to cover their needs (Prov. 30:8-9), yet to others this may look like health (2 Co.12:7-9). Sometimes it is hard to understand how God can deny us a particular job, when it will only allows us to give more and free up our schedule to do more things for Him. OR why He will not allow us the gift of a companion when He knows that it will only enable us to serve Him better. We must remember, however, that He is a good Father, hence, He only knows how to give good things to His children, even if it appears contrary to us. #RD #DR

13: This is a verse that all who do not know God could use as an attack to the concordance of the Bible, for we are told that the path that leads to eternal faith is narrow, whereas David here describes it as being wide. Truth is, that the path looks narrow only when we walk apart from it, for once one experiences the pleasure of living for God, He, as David says, makes it so wide, so pleasant indeed. (Psalm 18:36) #GR #DR

20: False prophets are not just called wolves, but wolves under sheep skin because, unless very careful and knowledgeable of the Word, it will result hard to unveil them. Most of these wolves know the Scriptures well enough to twist them and make a sermon that seems to agree with Scripture. Many of them quote more Scriptures in their talk than most genuine sheep. Because they know how to contort them to cover their behavior. And opposite to common thought, most of these wolves actually speak much truth about God and from God’s word. The can craft a message that is 98% true and 2% lies and still convince those who are not firm to follow them. However, even if their tongue does not give them away immediately, their actions will. A wolf can memorize truth, but it canot act upon it. Many times their behavior will betray them before their tongues do. #BR #DR
