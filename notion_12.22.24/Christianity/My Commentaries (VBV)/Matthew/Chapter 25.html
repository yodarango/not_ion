Chapter 25

10: Can the evil person blame God for excluding them from His Kingdom? Impossible! By this point, at the coming of the bridegroom that is, the sinner would have enjoyed plenteous opportunities for repentance. It was Christ who first stood outside their door waiting to be answered (Rev. 3:20). Yet, He was shot out. When this happens Jesus Christ will not be punishing those who shut Him outside their lives, He will only be respecting their decision. #BR #DR

13: The day and the hour, nobody knows. Just as farmer does not know the day and the hour that rain will fall on his crops. Nonetheless, he does know when the rain season approximates. For he has been trained in knowing the seasons. That is, of course, unless he has been a lazy farmer whose attention was lured by other things. When Christ returns He might come by surprise to those who do not await Him (1 Thes. 5:2), but He will not come unanticipated to whose foundation of their life is the preparation to receive Him (1 Thes. 5:4). #OR #DR

15: The Fact that He gave them distinct amounts initially tells us that these men were already some doing more than the others. God gives them according to their abilities. God will not ask us to do something that is out of our hands. [Philippians 2:13]. Many times God will asks us to do something difficult and if we do not have the Holy Spirit we will never be able to obey. Between the great things that we can’t do and the small things that we don’t want to do, there is a risk that we won’t do anything for the Lord”

16-17 God does not look at total quantities, He looks at percentages. both of the first men made 100% more of the original amount. They gave their 100% to the Lord, they did not live a half-way life. We can see the proof that God does not care about quantities but rather percentages when we look at the story of the widow and the two coins and the rich man. [Mark 12:41-44] The reason why God sees percentages and not amounts is because your giving translates to your love for God If I have a million dollars it won’t hurt to give $1,000 but if I am on minimum wage $1,000 might mean my rent and groceries for a month! Everything that God has given us is part of His wealth, the only way you can give Him 100% is by doing everything in Jesus name, in other words for His glory, whether is ministry or secular activities like your job. [Colossians 3:17] Everything Good has given us is more for His glory and the benefit of others than for my own. Talents, resources, knowledge, skills, intelligence, etc. It is all for His glory and with the purpose of helping others When we refuse to use what God has given us we are hiding the goodness of God to to others, specially if we are hiding it from unbelievers. A city on a hill can’t be hidden, neither a lamp is put under the bed [Matthew 5:14]. Do people at work know you are a Christian or are you digging holes?

17: In order to make more money out of the money you make you HAVE to take risks, that is how money works. If you invest it you might gain more or you might lose it. You might double it or you might get in debt. These two men here we risk takers. #LB #RLD

18: This was not only a lazy man he was a coward man. He did not like risks. He actually returned less money to the Master than what he started with considering inflation. #BR #RLD

18: Next time you halt yourself from doing something for fear of pride, failure, arrogance, inadequacy, etc. Just know that won’t excuse you before God. #GR #DR

21-23: The response for both of these servants is exactly word for word the same, how come? Because they both gave the same percentage: 100%. Once again this proves to us that God does not extrapolate our reward based on quantitates. He is a master of no favorites and He will rewards us according to our work not to our revenue. It is the Quality not the quantity, both these men put their all in it. [1 Corinthians 3:13] Say the story about the praying old lady and the big preacher who get to heaven by McCguee. It mattes with what heart we do things.

24: It is no surprise that the only man that did not work for the Lord was the only man with the wrong view about God! How you view God will determine how you serve Him. To him He was not a lovely Master, He was Just a hard man. #GR #DR

24: Is he calling His Lord a thief? makes sense. To this person the Lord should indeed come like a thief (1 Thes. 5:2)

24-25: Failing to have a relationship with God will determine what we do for Him. To this man He was not a kind and sweet Savior, it was just a “hard Man”. If we see God just as a hard Man waiting for us to fail so He can crush us we will never do anything for Him. Someone once said “I rather shoot for the stars and fail than shoot for the gutter and make it.” This man confess two sins, “I knew” and “I was afraid” both of which condemns: The coward will not inherit the kingdom of heaven [Revelations 21:8], and knowing and not doing is a sin and It wouldn’t have been better to never know the path of righteousness [1 Peter 2:21]. 25: This man perhaps took more time and definitely more effort in carving a whole than to simply take it to the bankers. The truth of the matter is that He didn’t love His master. He had gotten His part and didn’t care about making more profit for Him. It is very important where we spend our time. The things we do and the things we not do. Where are you investing your time today?

25: “I was afraid” that's why the cowardly are the first ones mentioned in the list. God wants no sissies [Rev 21:8].

26: You know the gospel, but you don't put it to work. You know the truth, but you don't follow it. You know the will of God, but you refuse It. That's why Peter says it would've been better if you hadn't known [2 Peter 2:21] 27: It MUCH easier to take the money to a banker than to cave a whole. In other words, this man worked more by doing the wrong thing. The truth of the matter Is that he didn't love his Lord.

27: Investing automatically involves risk, you don’t know whether you will make profit or lose all your initial resources. Working for God and investing what He has given us requires faith and boldness. However, if we trust God during the process we will be like the other two men who took the risk and were successful. Our spiritual live is like physical muscle which if not put under stress and nourished properly will eventually die. If God is not given us the gifts we want is because we haven’t put the use those we already have. Just as there is a reward for doing, there is a reward for not doing. At the end of our lives we will all Gert a reward from the Lord wether is good or bad. [Romans 2:6]

29: And you have not because you ask not and because you don't work it my beloved. That's why you don't have.

32: According to Vernon McGhee this verse refers to nations specifically for God never refers to people as goats.

41: Noticed that when He says that the Kingdom of heaven was prepared it was prepared for men. However, when He talks about Hell he refers to that as the place for Satan and his demons. You might be in you way to Hell, but that's not where God wants you. That is not the place He created for you.

44: God will not leave without proof their evilness to the evil one.

46: “They will go” look that nobody is being thrown into this punishment by God. They are going. Which implies it's something they are choosing, or in this case, chose to do. The words of my favorite evangelist, Yille Avila come I to my mind when he said “no one in hell can say that God grabbed them by the hair and thrust them in there. God does not send anyone to hell. Men choose that willingly.” In other words, God does not punched men into hell, men willingly jumps in there.
